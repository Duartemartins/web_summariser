            Text Analyser

            The Text Analyser provides real-time data, including word count, readability score, and sentiment analysis. Plus, you'll get a list of the top 5 most frequently used words along with their densities. Are you keen on examining your text in even greater depth? You can conveniently download a table of all words organised by frequency.

Crafting the perfect tweet? The Text Analyser is perfect for you. Monitor your word count to stay within Twitter's limit, and use the sentiment score to gauge the tone of your message. Making sure your tweets hit the right note has never been easier.

Analyse customer feedback, evaluate the readability of your technical documents, or optimise your content for SEO with ease. All you have to do is input your text and watch the comprehensive analytics unfold in real-time.
